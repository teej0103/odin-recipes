Moscow Mule

Description

This cocktail's origin has nothing to do with Moscow or mules, but after a few sips of this vodka, ginger beer, and lime concoction, no one will care.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lluid ounces vodka
  • 0.5 fluid ounces lime juice
  • ice cubes
  • 0.5 cup ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge for garnish

Directions

  1. Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug; add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine.
  2. Drop a lime wedge into the mug for garnish.
